The goal of this policy is to ensure that users know the difference between the official **Jend-notes** service (hosted by the creator) and third-party versions of the software.

* **Personal & Educational Use:** You are free to use the name to talk about the project, write tutorials, or show off your self-hosted instance.
* **No Confusion:** You may not use the name or logo in a way that implies an official partnership, endorsement, or that your version is the "official" version.
* **Commercial Services:** If you choose to host a commercial service based on the AGPLv3 source code, you **must** use a different name and logo. You cannot call your service "send-notes Hosting" or "The send-notes Cloud."

If you fork this project:

2. You must remove the official logo from the user interface and documentation.
